SGDC DISPLAY v1.0 RC2 — PARISH COUNCIL EDITION

BRANDING
- Introduces SGDC: St. George Digital Communications.
- The display component is SGDC Display.

HEADER
- Increased church/priest block indent.
- Enlarged cross to approximately 130% of RC1 size.
- Moved day/date/time inward to balance the left side.
- Increased time size.
- Converted Upcoming Events to a continuous stream with no dead time.

PANELS
- Announcements retained as approved.
- Stewardship plaque now has one fixed size for every fund.
- Plaque changes use a clean cut; no flashing, fading, or resizing.
- Stewardship bottom border removed.
- Parish Life slideshow reduced 25% and framed on all four sides with a 2px gold line.
- Added caption space below the slideshow.
- Today's Service and Hokehankist now share a two-column panel.
- Hokehankist names reduced to create more vertical room.
- Volunteers Needed retained and its bottom border removed.
- Featured Events now rotates several events with a quick cube transition and no border.
- Community converted to a continuous stream with no dead time.

DESIGN SYSTEM FOUNDATION
- All panel titles use gold.
- Title color and size are defined as panel variables for future Layout Manager control.
- Panel borders remain 2px and are applied independently by class.
- Image framing is separate from panel framing.

SCALING
- Desktop and television remain anchored and stable.
- Phone portrait and landscape use proportional fit and centering.
- No cropping or panel rearrangement on phones.
